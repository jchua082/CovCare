Travel Requirements

Updated on: 2 Nov 2022

Written by: John Tan

Canâ€™t wait to go for a vacation? Although most countries have lifted travelling restriction, here are some places where COVID-19 travel restrictions are still applicable.

China

Who can enter the country?

The Singapore-China Fast Lane for essential business and official travel between Singapore and China is between Singapore and six Chinese provinces/municipalities (Chongqing, Guangdong, Jiangsu, Shanghai, Tianjin and Zhejiang). Singaporeans seeking to travel to China under the Fast Lane must be sponsored by either a company or a government agency in China, which will file an application on behalf of the applicant with the local province/municipal authorities. More details on the application process and requirements of the Singapore-China Fast Lane can be found here.


PCR Testing Requirement

Travellers are required to perform 2 pre-departure PCR tests before entering China. You can get your PCR test conveniently at CovCare.


Quarantine Measures

As of 28 June 2022, the quarantine measures for inbound travellers to Mainland China have been reduced to 7 days of centralised quarantine, followed by 3 days of home monitoring for most cities. Before travelling, Singaporeans are strongly advised to check with the local authorities, as well as the hotel or residential compound where they will be staying, on the quarantine, COVID-19 testing, and other policies that they should adhere to when entering China.


Mask Wearing Mandate

Masks are required to be worn at all times in public.



Hong Kong

Who can enter the country?

For non-Hong Kong residents aged 12 or above who have stayed in overseas places, only persons who have fulfilled vaccination requirement are allowed to board a flight to Hong Kong.


PCR Testing Requirement

Travellers will have to conduct a self-arranged Antigen Rapid Test (ART) within 24 hours prior to the scheduled time of departure and submit their negative results via the online Health Declaration Form. Traveller are required to monitor and keep photos of ART test results 7 days upon arrival. The ART test can be self-administered or by professional swab sampling.


Quarantine Measures

Travellers are required to quarantine for 7 days upon arrival.


Mask Wearing Mandate

Masks are required to be worn at all times in public.
