Our Vision

At CovCare, people come first. Our organization has been built off of trust, unity, accountability, service excellence and dedication. We believe that anyone from all walks of life deserves the chance to be tested as communal health is imperative in every instance including what may happen if there was another pandemic. When it comes down to our health - there simply isn't time for waiting!

Our Mission

As a leader in Covid-19 testing, we are committed to make infectious disease testing accurate, accessible, and affordable for everyone. Our team of professionals has made it our mission to provide quality integrated testing services for our clients in a caring and efficient manner to remove the complexities and anxiety of diagnostic tests.

Our Team




The Founder and CEO

Founded in 2020, CovCare, a specialised PCR Testing centre was established by Dr. Jane Lim. She graduated from NUS School of Medicine specialising in Infectious Diseases and was the Senior Consultant at Singapore General Hospital for 6 years.
To help increase the capacity for regular testing for COVID-19, Dr Lim founded CovCare. With this specialised and affordable service, we hope to encourage Singaporeans to test themselves regularly to protect themselves and the people around them.




The Medical Team

Our medical team consists of 50 professional staff members with varied responsibilities from administrative staff, healthcare support workers to laboratory staff. All of our medical staff members are certified healthcare professionals with valid practising certifications issued by the Singapore Medical Council (SMC). As a team, we strive to deliver the most comfortable testing experience and ensure accurate testing results.




The Logistics Team

Our logistics team ensures that our supplies are always readily available and makes sure that all our equipment is properly sanitised before and after testing to protect our patients and staff. We also make sure that testing results are reliable and accurate through multiple comprehensive internal and external quality assurance programmes that are set in place to ensure high-quality patient testing.
