We are a MOH-Approved Test Centre!

CovCare is a licensed provider of a wide range of PCR or Serology tests for COVID-19, all of which has been approved by the Ministry of Health. All our COVID-19 swab and serology tests are approved by HSA Laboratories and administered by certified healthcare professionals.

Whether you are travelling for pleasure or business, serving a Stay-At-Home Notice (SHN) or would like to be voluntarily tested for peace of mind - we're here to offer COVID-19 tests for all those with unique needs in the convenience of your own time and place.

Accurate

Our tests can guarantee 99.9% accuracy in results, and our fast turnaround times are unrivalled.

Accessible

Our tests can be taken at your own convenience, and they're available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week!

Affordable

Our tests are tailored for every budget - whether you can afford them now or if you'd rather pay later, we've got you covered.

Our Simple Procedures

1. Make a booking/walk-in

Select a time slot online or you can walk in to any of our centres *waiting time is subjected to centre capacity.

2. Register and Pay

Confirm your details with us and proceed to make payment. We accept test first pay later and instalments are welcomed too.

4. Wait for results

Receive notarised results and documentations via email within 3 hours.

3. Relax and get tested

We will prepare everything for you, all you have to do is relax and wait to be tested by our qualified healthcare professionals
